About Serengeti Trail Adventures

Founded in 2014, Serengeti Trail Adventures is a family-owned tour operator based in Arusha, specializing in authentic wildlife experiences across Tanzania's northern circuit. We believe that a safari is not just a trip — it is a transformational encounter with the natural world.

All our guides grew up in Tanzania and speak the land as their mother tongue. We operate 12 vehicles, employ 18 certified guides, and have zero disputes across 127 SafariGo-verified trips. We joined SafariGo's network in early 2025 and earned our Elite Escrow Performer badge in Q4 of the same year.
